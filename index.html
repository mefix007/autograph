Ogun State, Nigeria

Ogun State in Nigeria stands adorned with a plethora of natural endowments, including limestone deposits, crude oil reservoirs, and flourishing rubber tree plantations, among others. Its capital city, Abeokuta, derives its name from the profound geological feature it houses, signifying "under the rock." This nomenclature finds its roots in a colossal rock that served as a concealed refuge for ancestral inhabitants during times of conflict.
